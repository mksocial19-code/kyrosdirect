What We Do

Full-Scope Commercial Concrete

We handle the complete concrete scope on commercial projects — ground preparation through final finish. No subcontracting the core work. Every crew on site is ours.

01
Paving

Concrete paving for parking lots, access drives, loading docks, and commercial roadways. Built to handle heavy commercial loads and North Texas weather conditions over the long term.

02
Slab on Grade

Ground-level structural slabs for warehouses, distribution centers, retail buildings, and industrial facilities. Designed and placed to engineering specifications with proper sub-base preparation.

03
Piers

Drilled concrete piers for commercial foundations in the expansive clay soils common across the DFW area. Sized and placed to structural engineering requirements on every project.

04
Sidewalks

Commercial sidewalks for new construction and renovations. Properly formed, reinforced, and finished to meet municipal standards and project specifications from day one.

05
Structural Concrete

Walls, columns, grade beams, and elevated deck work for commercial and industrial structures. We work from engineered drawings and hold to spec through every pour.

06
Integral Colored Decorative Concrete

Color and texture built into the concrete mix, not applied on top. Purpose-built for high-traffic commercial environments where durability and aesthetics both matter long term.

07
ADA Compliance

Ramps, curb cuts, detectable warning surfaces, and accessible route construction meeting ADA and TAS requirements. New construction and retrofit work on existing facilities.

About Trillium

Built on 15 Years of Earned Work

Trillium Construction Company has operated out of Garland, Texas since 2008. We are a turnkey commercial concrete contractor — meaning we self-perform the work, manage the schedule, and stand behind every pour without passing accountability down to subcontractors.

Our typical project runs $500,000 to $2,500,000. That range reflects work on commercial complexes, industrial facilities, municipal projects, and institutional construction throughout the Dallas area and North Texas. We are fully insured and bondable up to $5 million.

General contractors and project owners bring us back because we show up prepared, communicate without drama, and do not leave problems for someone else to resolve. That is the whole operation.

  • HUB Certified — State of Texas Historically Underutilized Business certification through the Texas Comptroller of Public Accounts, recognized on state and municipal public projects.
  • MBE Certified — State of Texas Minority Business Enterprise certification through the State of Texas. Counts toward diversity requirements on public and institutional projects.
  • Bondable to $5 Million Fully insured and bondable up to $5 million. General contractors can bring us onto bonded projects with confidence in our capacity to perform and be held accountable.
  • In Business Since 2008 Over 15 years of commercial concrete work across the Dallas-Fort Worth area. A track record on public and private sector projects that speaks for itself.
